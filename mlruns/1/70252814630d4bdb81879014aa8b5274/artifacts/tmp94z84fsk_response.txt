If your car gets damaged on the way to work, follow these steps based on the company car policy:

- **Report the Damage**: You must report any damage to the vehicle within 24 hours. 
    - Email: hr@easi.net
    - Include:
        - Description of the incident
        - Photographs of the damaged areas
        - Date and place of the incident
        - Police or insurer reference number (if applicable)

- **Consequences of Late Reporting**: Be aware that failing to report the damage within 24 hours may result in you having to cover part of the repair cost.

- **Mileage Log**: If traveling home to the office or directly to a client location, ensure to log it separately as it qualifies for professional mileage.

These steps are critical to ensuring proper handling of the situation and minimizing any potential costs to yourself.
