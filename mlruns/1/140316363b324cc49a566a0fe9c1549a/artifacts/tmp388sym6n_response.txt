Here’s what happens to your unused vacation days at the end of the year:

- **Carry Over**: You can carry over a maximum of 5 unused vacation days to the next calendar year.
- **Expiration**: These carried-over days will expire on 31 March of the following year.
- **Payout**: Carried-over days cannot be paid out.
- **Reminder**: HR will send a reminder in early February if you have any carried-over days remaining.

It's recommended to schedule your leave throughout the year to prevent losing any accrued days.
