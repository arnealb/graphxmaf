You can filter SmartSales catalog items by the following fields:

### Text Fields
- **fulltext**
- **code**
- **title**
- **description**
- **measurementUnitUid**
- **tag**
- **availability**
- **unitofmeasure**
- **group**
- **Client - Supplies to** (related to specific client)
- **Client - Headquarter** (related to specific client)
- **Client - Headquarter of** (related to specific client)
- **Client - Billing company of** (related to specific client)

### Date/Time Fields
- **techServerCreationDate**
- **techServerUpdateDate**
- **since**
- **contextualDate** (hidden field)

### Boolean Fields
- **deleted**
- **hideEmptyGroups**
- **includeContextualDisplayableRelationChildrenOf**

### Decimal Fields
- **price**
- **measure**

### Integer Fields
- **salesunit**
- **packagingunit**

### Other Fields
- **groupUid** (hidden field)
- **layoutUid** (hidden field)
- **linkParentUid** (hidden field)
- **includeDisplayableRelationChildrenOf** (hidden field)
- **contextualLocationUid** (hidden field)

### Notes
- Some fields are marked as hidden and may not be visible in typical user interfaces.
- Certain client-related fields are designated for specific relationships within the catalog. 

These fields provide a variety of options for filtering catalog items based on text, date, boolean, numerical, and relation attributes.
