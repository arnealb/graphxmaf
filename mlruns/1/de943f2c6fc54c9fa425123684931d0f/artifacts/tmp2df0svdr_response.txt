In the event of a complete disruption of the SmartSales platform for Colruyt, follow this notification protocol:

### First Notification
- **Who to Notify First**: 
  - Arne Albrecht (a.albrecht@easi.net)
  - Dorian Feaux (d.feaux@easi.net)
  - EASI Delivery Manager

### Timeframe for Notification
- **Critical Incidents (S1)**: Immediate notification 
- **High Incidents (S2)**: Notify Arne Albrecht within 30 minutes

### Escalation
- If the incident is not resolved within 2 hours, the EASI Chief Operating Officer will be notified.

This process is designed to ensure effective communication and rapid response during critical incidents.
