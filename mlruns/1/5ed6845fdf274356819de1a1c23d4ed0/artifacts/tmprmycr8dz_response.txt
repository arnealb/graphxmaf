Here are the findings regarding your company car and non-compete clause at EASI:

### Company Car Policy for Employees Leaving EASI
- **Returning the Vehicle**: 
  - Must be returned on your last working day to EASI reception at Avenue Louise 65.
  - Return all keys, registration documents, fuel/charging cards, and accessories.
  
- **Final Mileage Log**: 
  - A final mileage log must be submitted on the same day the vehicle is returned.

- **Damage Reporting**: 
  - Any unreported damage to the vehicle will be charged to you based on the lease company’s damage assessment.

- **Compliance with Offboarding Procedures**: 
  - Follow the rules outlined in the Company Car Policy, including damage reporting and mileage log submission.

### Non-Compete Clause at EASI
- **Duration**: 12 months from your last working day.
- **Geographic Scope**: Belgium.
- **Sectoral Scope**: Covers field sales software, CRM implementation, and retail analytics.
- **Compensation**: 50% of your last gross monthly salary, paid for the duration of the clause.
- **Waiver**: EASI may waive the clause in writing within 15 days of your resignation, which would remove both the restriction and the compensation.
