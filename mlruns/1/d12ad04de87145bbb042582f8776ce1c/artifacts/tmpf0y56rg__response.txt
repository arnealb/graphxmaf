You can sort SmartSales orders by the following fields:

- **Creation Dates**
  - techServerCreationDate
  - techClientCreationDate

- **Update Dates**
  - techServerUpdateDate
  - techClientUpdateDate
  - approbationModificationDate

- **Order Details**
  - date
  - versionKey
  - hasManualDiscount
  - user
  - customerCode
  - customer
  - locationExternalId
  - total
  - totalWithoutDiscount
  - totalDiscount
  - totalDiscountPercentage

These fields are available for sorting on both WEB and MOBILE platforms (where indicated).
