Here is a summary of the notification steps and deadlines for the data breach involving Colruyt customer data:

### Notification Steps and Deadlines
- **Internal Reporting Obligation**:
  - Report the breach within **2 hours** to the Data Protection Officer (DPO) at privacy@easi.net or dpo@easi.net.

- **Initial Assessment**:
  - Complete by the DPO and IT Security team within **4 hours** of the initial report.

- **Notification to the Belgian Data Protection Authority (DPA)**:
  - Notify the DPA within **72 hours** if the assessment indicates a risk to data subjects.

- **Notification to Data Subjects**:
  - Communicate directly with affected individuals if the breach poses a high risk to their rights and freedoms.

- **Notification to Client Controllers**:
  - Notify affected clients within **24 hours** if the breach involves data processed on their behalf.

These steps are critical for ensuring compliance and timely communication regarding data breaches.
