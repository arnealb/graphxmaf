You can sort SmartSales catalog items by the following fields:

- **Date Fields**:
  - `techServerCreationDate`
  - `techServerUpdateDate`

- **Identification**:
  - `code`
  - `externalId`
  - `title`
  - `searchKeywordText`

- **Pricing**:
  - `price`

- **Measurement**:
  - `measurementUnitReferenceUnitCode`
  - `measurementUnitTitle`
  - `unitofmeasure`
  - `measure`

- **Positioning**:
  - `position`
  - `group`

- **Sales Information**:
  - `quantitySold`
  - `salesunit`
  - `packagingunit`

- **Descriptions**:
  - `description`
  - `availability`

These fields are accessible via both web and mobile platforms.
