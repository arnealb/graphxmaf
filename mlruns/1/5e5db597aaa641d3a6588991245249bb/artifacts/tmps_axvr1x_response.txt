### Steps to Follow:

   - Move your vehicle to a safe location if possible.

   - Look for any possible injuries to yourself or other individuals involved.

3. **Contact Authorities**:
   - If there is significant damage or injuries, call law enforcement to report the incident.

   - Report the incident to your insurance provider as soon as possible to initiate claims.
