Here’s how to report a suspected phishing email and the consequences of failing a phishing test twice:

### Reporting a Suspected Phishing Email:
- **Procedure**: 
  - Report immediately through the security portal at [security.easi.net](http://security.easi.net) or by emailing security@easi.net.
  - Do not investigate independently or clean the affected device, as this may lead to loss of evidence.
  - If unsure, contact trained first responders Arne Albrecht or Dorian Feaux for assistance.

### Consequences of Failing a Phishing Test:
- If an employee fails a phishing test twice within a 12-month period, it will result in a discussion with HR. 

These guidelines ensure both the safety of the organization and the accountability of employees regarding security measures.
