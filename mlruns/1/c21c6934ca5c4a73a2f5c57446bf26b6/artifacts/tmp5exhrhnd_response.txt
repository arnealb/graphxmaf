From SmartSales, the following fields are available to display in the location list:

### Date/Time Fields
- **techServerCreationDate** (DATETIME)
- **techServerUpdateDate** (DATETIME)
- **techClientCreationDate** (DATETIME)
- **techClientUpdateDate** (DATETIME)
- **lastVisitDate** (DATETIME)
- **lastOrderDate** (DATETIME)
- **extranetStartDate** (DATE)
- **extranetEndDate** (DATE)

### Text Fields
- **techCreationUser** (TEXT)
- **techUpdateUser** (TEXT)
- **code** (TEXT)
- **externalId** (TEXT)
- **name** (TEXT)
- **street** (TEXT)
- **city** (TEXT)
- **zip** (TEXT)
- **country** (TEXT)
- **vatNumber** (TEXT)
- **extranetUsername** (TEXT)
- **extranetLanguage** (TEXT)
- **email** (TEXT)
- **Supplied by** (TEXT - Children relation)
- **Headquarter of** (TEXT - Children relation)
- **Headquarter** (TEXT - Parents relation)
- **Billing company** (TEXT - Children relation)
- **VAT** (TEXT)

### Boolean Fields
- **commented** (BOOLEAN)
- **hasExtranetAccess** (BOOLEAN)

### Decimal Fields
- **latitude** (DECIMAL)
- **longitude** (DECIMAL)

These fields can be used across WEB and MOBILE audiences as specified.
