Here’s how to report a suspected phishing email and the consequences of failing a phishing test twice:

### Reporting a Suspected Phishing Email
- **Procedure**: 
  - Use the "Report Phishing" button in Outlook for all suspected phishing attempts received in EASI mailboxes.
  - If the "Report Phishing" button is not available, send a report via email to security@easi.net.
  - **Important**: Do not investigate independently or attempt to "clean" the affected device, as this may compromise evidence.
- **Review Time**: The IT security team will review each report within 4 working hours.

*Source: IT Security Procedure (EASI)*

### Consequences of Failing a Phishing Test Twice
- If an employee fails a phishing test twice within a 12-month period, this will trigger a discussion with HR.

*Source: IT Security Procedure (EASI)*
