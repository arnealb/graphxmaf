### Notification Process:
- **Immediate Reporting**:
  - Report the incident via:
    - SmartSales client portal
    - Email: support@easi.net
    - Phone (for S1 and S2 incidents)

- **Initial Notifications (for S1 incidents)**:
  - Notify the following individuals immediately:
    - Arne Albrecht: a.albrecht@easi.net
    - Dorian Feaux: d.feaux@easi.net
    - EASI Delivery Manager

- **Escalation**:
  - If the issue is unresolved in **2 hours**, notify the EASI Chief Operating Officer, who will join the response team.

- **Client Communication**:
  - Arne Albrecht (EASI Account Manager for Colruyt Group) will provide status updates to the client contact every **2 hours** until the incident is resolved. 

This process ensures prompt and effective communication during a disruption.
