At EASI, the policy regarding working from home during your probation period is as follows:

- **Remote Work During Probation**: Remote work is not permitted during the first 6 months of employment.
- **On-Site Requirement**: New joiners are required to be on-site full time to absorb working practices, build relationships, and become productive.
- **Post-Probation**: After successfully completing your probation period, you will be eligible for the standard hybrid work arrangement.
