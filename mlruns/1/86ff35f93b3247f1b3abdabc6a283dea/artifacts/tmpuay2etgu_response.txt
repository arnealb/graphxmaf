Here are the notification steps and deadlines for addressing a data breach involving customer data at EASI:

1. **Internal Reporting Obligation**:
   - Employees must report any suspected or discovered personal data breach within **2 hours** to the EASI Data Protection Officer (DPO) at privacy@easi.net or dpo@easi.net.

2. **Initial Assessment**:
   - The DPO and the IT Security team will conduct an initial assessment within **4 hours** of the internal report to evaluate the nature and impact of the breach.

3. **Notification to the Belgian Data Protection Authority (DPA)**:
   - If the assessment indicates a risk to data subjects, EASI must inform the Belgian DPA within **72 hours** of becoming aware of the breach.

4. **Notification to Data Subjects**:
   - If the breach poses a high risk to the rights and freedoms of data subjects, EASI is required to communicate the breach directly to the affected individuals.

5. **Notification to Client Controllers**:
   - If the breach involves data processed on behalf of a client, EASI must notify the affected client within **24 hours** of identifying the breach.

These steps are essential for ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and timely communication related to data breaches.
