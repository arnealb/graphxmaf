### Financial Consequences for EASI if P1 Response SLA is Missed for Colruyt

- **Service Credit:** If EASI fails to meet the response target for a P1 incident:
  - A service credit of **10% of the monthly fee** for that month is applied.
  - Multiple breaches in the same month may be aggregated.
  - However, the total service credit per month is **capped at 30%** of the monthly fee.

- **Application of Service Credits:** 
  - Service credits are applied as a deduction on the next invoice.
  - These credits are the **sole financial remedy** for missed support commitments under the SLA, with exceptions for cases of gross negligence or willful misconduct.

- **Other Notes:**
  - There are no specified financial consequences for Colruyt regarding missed P1 response SLAs in the provided context.
