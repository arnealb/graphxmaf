If EASI misses the P1 response SLA for Colruyt, the financial consequences are as follows:

- **Service Credit**: A service credit of **10% of the monthly fee** will be applied for that month for each missed P1 response.
- **Aggregation**: If multiple P1 breaches occur in the same month, the service credits will be aggregated.
- **Cap**: However, the total service credit per month is capped at **30% of the monthly fee**.
- **Application of Credits**: These service credits will be deducted from the next invoice.
- **Exclusivity**: Service credits are the sole financial remedy for missed commitments under the SLA, except in cases of gross negligence or willful misconduct.

This information is derived from the **SLA agreement (EASI - Colruyt)** and associated documents.
