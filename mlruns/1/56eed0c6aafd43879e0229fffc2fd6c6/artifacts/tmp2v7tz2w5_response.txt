Here is a summary of the notification steps and deadlines following the discovery of a data breach involving Colruyt customer data:

1. **Internal Reporting**:
   - EASI employees must report the breach within **2 hours** to the EASI Data Protection Officer (DPO) via privacy@easi.net or dpo@easi.net.

2. **Notification to Clients**:
   - EASI must notify affected clients within **24 hours** of identifying the breach, regardless of whether notification to the Data Protection Authority (DPA) is required.

3. **Notification to the Belgian DPA**:
   - If a risk to data subjects is identified, EASI is required to notify the Belgian DPA within **72 hours** of becoming aware of the breach.

4. **Notification to Data Subjects**:
   - If the breach poses a high risk to the rights and freedoms of data subjects, EASI must communicate the breach directly to the affected individuals as soon as possible after identification.

These steps are crucial for compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and for safeguarding the rights of individuals affected by the breach.
