### Summary of Company Car Policy and Non-Compete Clause at EASI

#### Company Car Policy
- **Eligibility**: Employees needing regular travel or with cars included in their compensation package qualify.
  - **Categories**:
    - **Category A**: Compact electric/hybrid for consultants and analysts (less than 3 years seniority).
    - **Category B**: Mid-range for senior consultants, Sales Engineers, team leads, account managers.
    - **Category C**: Premium models for managers and directors.
- **Fuel Card Usage**: Issued for professional use; a €150 monthly contribution is required for private use.
- **Professional Mileage**: Travel between the office and client locations counts as professional.
- **Replacement**: Vehicles are replaced every 4 years or at 120,000 km; notification is given three months prior.
- **Returning Vehicles**: Must be returned on the last working day with all related items.
- **Damage Reporting**: All vehicle damage must be reported within 24 hours.
- **Insurance**: Covered under EASI's fleet insurance; specific procedures for accidents must be followed.

#### Non-Compete Clause
- **Applicable Roles**: Applies to positions such as Sales Engineer, Business Analyst, and Customer Success Manager.
- **Duration**: 12 months post-employment.
- **Geographic Scope**: Limited to Belgium.
- **Sectoral Scope**: Concerns field sales software, CRM implementation, and retail analytics.
- **Compensation**: 50% of the last gross monthly salary during the clause's duration.
- **Waiver Option**: EASI can waive the non-compete clause in writing within 15 days of resignation, removing restrictions and compensation obligations.
