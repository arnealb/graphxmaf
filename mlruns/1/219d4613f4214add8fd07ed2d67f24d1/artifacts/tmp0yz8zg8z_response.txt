Here’s what happens to your unused vacation days at the end of the year:

- You can carry over a maximum of **5 unused leave days** to the following calendar year.
- These carried-over days **expire on 31 March** of that year.
- The unused days **cannot be paid out**.
- It is recommended to plan your leave during the year to avoid losing accrued days.
- **HR sends a reminder** in early February to inform employees of any carried-over days remaining.
