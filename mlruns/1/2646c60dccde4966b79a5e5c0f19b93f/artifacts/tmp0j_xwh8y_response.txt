Here's what you need to know regarding your company car and the non-compete clause as you prepare to leave EASI:

### Company Car
- **Return Process**: You must return the company car on your last working day, along with all related items.
- **Personal Contribution**: If you have been using the car for personal purposes, you may have a personal contribution of EUR 150 per month for that use.
- **Professional Mileage**: Remember that travel between the EASI office and client locations counts as professional mileage.
- **Condition Reporting**: Any damages to the vehicle need to be reported within 24 hours.

### Non-Compete Clause
- **Applicability**: The non-compete clause applies to your role, as you are likely in a commercial or customer-facing position.
- **Duration**: The clause lasts for 12 months from your last working day.
- **Geographical Scope**: It covers the territory of Belgium.
- **Sectoral Scope**: It applies specifically to the field of sales software, CRM implementation, and retail analytics.
- **Compensation**: You will receive 50% of your last gross monthly salary for the duration of this clause.
- **Waiver Option**: EASI may waive the non-compete clause in writing within 15 days of your resignation, which would remove both the restrictions and the compensation.
