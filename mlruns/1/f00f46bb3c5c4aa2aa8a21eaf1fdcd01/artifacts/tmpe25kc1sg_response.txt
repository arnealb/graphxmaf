At EASI, you are entitled to:

- **20 statutory vacation days** per calendar year.
- **12 ADV days** (reduction of working hours).

This totals **32 paid leave days** for a full-time, full-year employee. For part-time staff and those who join or leave mid-year, vacation days are accrued on a pro-rata basis.
