Here are the password requirements and change frequency for EASI:

### Password Requirements:
- Minimum length of **14 characters**
- Must include:
  - At least **one uppercase letter**
  - At least **one lowercase letter**
  - At least **one number**
  - At least **one special character**
- Cannot be the same as any of the last **12 passwords used**.

### Change Frequency:
- Passwords must be rotated at least every **90 days**.

Additionally, multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all EASI systems.
