You can sort SmartSales orders by the following fields:

- **Creation Dates:**
  - techServerCreationDate
  - techClientCreationDate

- **Update Dates:**
  - techServerUpdateDate
  - techClientUpdateDate
  - approbationModificationDate

- **Order Details:**
  - date
  - versionKey
  - total
  - totalWithoutDiscount
  - totalDiscount
  - totalDiscountPercentage

- **Customer Information:**
  - user
  - customerCode
  - customer
  - locationExternalId

- **Discount Information:**
  - hasManualDiscount

These sortable fields are accessible on the WEB and MOBILE audiences, though some are limited to WEB.
