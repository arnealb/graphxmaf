- **unitofmeasure**

### Date/Time Fields

- **techServerUpdateDate**
- **since**

- **hideEmptyGroups**
- **includeContextualDisplayableRelationChildrenOf**

### Numeric Fields
- **price**
- **salesunit**
- **packagingunit**

Some fields are hidden and may not be visible in all contexts. Each field allows for specific types of queries, either enabling expressions or restricting their use.
