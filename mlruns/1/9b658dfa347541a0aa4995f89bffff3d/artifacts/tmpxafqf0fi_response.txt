Here are the password requirements and change frequency for EASI:

### Password Requirements:
- Minimum length of 14 characters.
- Must include at least:
  - One uppercase letter
  - One lowercase letter
  - One number
  - One special character
- Cannot be identical to any of the last 12 passwords used.
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all EASI systems.

### Password Change Frequency:
- Passwords must be rotated at least every 90 days. 

These details were obtained from the EASI IT Security Procedure and related documents.
