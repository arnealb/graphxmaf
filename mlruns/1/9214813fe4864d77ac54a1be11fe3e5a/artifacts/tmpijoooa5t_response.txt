At EASI, the vacation policy is as follows:

- **Total Vacation Days**: Each employee is entitled to 20 statutory vacation days plus 12 ADV days, totaling **32 paid leave days** per calendar year for full-time employees.
- **Part-Time and Mid-Year Employees**: Leave is accrued on a pro-rata basis.
- **Leave Requests**: Must be submitted through the HR portal, with specific notice periods required based on the leave length.
- **Carry Over Policy**: A maximum of 5 unused leave days can be carried over to the next calendar year but will expire on **31 March** of that year.

For further details, employees can reach out to HR at hr@easi.net.
